5 Export Formats

PNG spritesheet, OBJ+MTL for Blender, GLB for Unity, VOX for MagicaVoxel, JSON for custom pipelines. All from the Render Studio.

6 View Projections

Edit from all 6 sides — Front, Back, Left, Right, Top, Bottom. All views share the same voxel grid. Depth-aware with signed offset labels.

Simple workflow

From pixel to 3D in minutes.

01

Draw in 2D

Open the canvas, pick colors, draw with pencil, shapes, or flood fill. Exactly like any pixel art editor.

02

Set Depth

Adjust the depth slider and direction — Both, Front, or Back. Watch the 3D preview update live with every stroke.

03

Apply Materials

Assign Neon, Metal, Glass, or Emissive per color. The bloom post-processing responds in real time.

04

Export

Open Render Studio. Choose camera angle, lighting preset, adjust bloom, then download in your format of choice.

Materials & effects

Five material types. One click.

Assign any material to any color in your palette. The 3D model updates instantly.

Solid

Flat vertex-lit color. The default. Clean and fast.

Emissive

Self-lit, slightly boosted. Subtle ambient glow that passes bloom threshold.

Neon

Heavily boosted toward white. Full UnrealBloom glow. Made for synthwave aesthetics.

Metal

MeshStandardMaterial with high metalness (0.88) and low roughness. Specular highlights respond to scene lighting.

Glass

Semi-transparent and double-sided. Glass voxels don't occlude neighbors — see through them in 3D.
